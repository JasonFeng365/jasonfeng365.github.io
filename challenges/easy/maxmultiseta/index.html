Max Multiset A

Featured on SCPE Fall 2023

Max Multiset A is a deceptively easy problem. You are given an array of numbers, say [2, 2, -3], and must find the maximum sum of all of its multisets.
In this case, the answer would be 4, from the multiset {2, 2}.
During the SCPE, every single team was able to solve this problem!
